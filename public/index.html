Why Agent SCOCI

Built around what matters to you

Most health apps track your numbers. Agent SCOCI tracks your life — your ability to cook, drive, care for family, and do the things that give your days meaning.

Grounded in occupational therapy science

Agent SCOCI is built on the Middle-Range Theory of Self-Care of Chronic Illness (Riegel et al.) and OT's occupation-centered practice framework — the same evidence base used in clinical OT practice, now in your pocket between appointments.

How it works

From intake to insight in minutes

A one-time 7-step profile sets up your personalized SCOCI agent. Then it's there whenever you need it — online or offline.

1

Diagnosis & medications

Share your condition, how long you've had it, and what you're currently taking.

2

ADL assessment

Rate your independence in bathing, dressing, mobility, eating, and other basic self-care tasks.

3

IADL assessment

Rate your ability to manage meals, medications, driving, finances, home, and community life.

4

Occupational profile

Share the roles and activities that give your life meaning — and which ones your illness has disrupted.

5

Routine, environment & support

Describe your home setup, adaptive equipment, daily schedule, and support network.

6

Care team

Add your OT, physician, and specialists so SCOCI reinforces your clinical goals between visits.

Get the app

Install it like an app. No app store needed.

Agent SCOCI is a Progressive Web App — add it to your home screen directly from your browser. Works on iPhone, Android, and desktop. Works offline once installed.

Agent SCOCI — free to install

Your profile and all health data are saved only on your device. Completely private, no account required to get started.

iPhone / iPadSafari → tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
AndroidChrome → tap Menu → "Add to Home Screen"
DesktopChrome or Edge → install icon in address bar
Open Agent SCOCI

HIPAA-conscious architecture

In local PWA mode, Agent SCOCI stores your health profile and AI conversation history exclusively in your device's local storage using IndexedDB. Nothing is transmitted to any external server. Your health information is private by design — not just by policy.
